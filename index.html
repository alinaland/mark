A bit about me

Hey there, my name is Mark. I am a high school student living in New York. I love exploring hidden spots all over New York and photographing my friends. I live in Brighton Beach and for those who don't know it, it's a very colorful part of New York - one of the last real ethnic enclaves of New York City. I am drawn to strong colors, dramatic landscapes, and light. I use Sony a7iii, it's a great camera because it is fundamentally very easy to use, you never run out of space and the colors come out great. I have worked with various organizations and photographed large group events - weddings, parties, graduations, anniversaries - you name it! I enjoy photography because it gives me the opportunity to connect with different people and capture memorable moments. Check out my work below and let's get in touch!
